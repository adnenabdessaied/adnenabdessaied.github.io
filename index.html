Adnen Abdessaied

I am a first-year PhD student at the University of Stuttgart, Germany, advised by Prof. Andreas Bulling. My research focuses on mutli-modal exaplainable deep models at the intersection of Computer Vision and Natural Language Processing . In my work, I speciafically develop novel (neuro-symbolic) AI models for conversational tasks such as VQA, VideoQA, visual dialog, and video dialog. I hold a bachelor's and a master's degree in the elite study program Simulation Technology at the University of Stuttgart. The latter was obtained with distiction and received the outsanding work award from the Industrial Consortium SimTech (IC SimTech). During my studies, I did two AI-centric internships at Bosch.
